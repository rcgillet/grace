Elementary

Elementary students enjoy Bible classes each day and chapel once a week. Our elementary curriculum in the lower elementary is primarily A Beka; which at its core includes an intensive phonics program for reading instruction. Extra-curricular classes include gym twice a week, music twice a week, and German once a week. Upper elementary students learn to play the recorder. Elementary students perform two programs per year: one at Christmas and one in the spring. We offer an 8-10 week afterschool art program each year. In the spring students plant a raised- bed garden and through the summer parents do some harvesting. In the fall they ‘cook’ from their produce! Fifth Grade girls have the opportunity to play Junior High Volleyball.
