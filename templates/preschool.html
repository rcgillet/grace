Preschool Program

Children socialize and learn through a variety of age-appropriate activities, with an emphasis in the 4 yr. old program on Kindergarten readiness. Preschoolers are exposed daily to numbers, letters and other basic concepts. There will be a short seat work time each day, however, most learning will be done through play and age appropriate group activities. The 3 yr. old preschool meets Tuesday & Thursday mornings most years; 4 yr. old preschool meets Monday, Wednesday, and Friday mornings. Both offer optional afternoon care in years where the demand is sufficient.
