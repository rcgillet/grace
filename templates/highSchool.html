High School

Biblical Worldview

Realizing that the Word of God is the ultimate authority in the world, each class is taught from a Biblical perspective. Students are taught to compare and evaluate what they learn and hear to what the Bible says.

Classes

Students have all the traditional core classes that are typically offered in school—English, Science, History, Math, and Bible. Electives include but are not limited to PE, Personal finance, Cooking and Baking, and Computers.
