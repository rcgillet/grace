Who We Are

Grace Christian School is the only private, non-denominational Christian School in the Saginaw area. Founded in 1974 as a ministry of Grace Baptist Church, we educate children from 3 year-old preschool through twelfth grade with a Biblical world view. Our students and staff come from over 15 different area churches. We are located at 4619 Mackinaw Road in Saginaw Township. Our mission, “To know Jesus Christ and make Him known” is accomplished through biblically-integrated courses, excellence in academic endeavors, and practical ministry opportunities.



Enrollment

Enrollment Process
1)
Fill out the admissions form, and give the family reference form to your pastor, youth pastor, or Sunday school teacher to fill out.

2)
Turn in admissions form with enrollment fee; make an appointment to meet with the principal. Students are encouraged to come; 7-12 graders are required to come.

3)
Read the student handbook and discuss it with your child(ren) in an age-appropriate manner.

4)
The meeting with the principal will give you opportunity to ask questions and get to know who we are, take a tour of the school, determine if the student will require admissions testing, decide on billing options, and discuss expectations.

5)
An additional conference or conversation may be required after testing or receipt of school records. All new students are accepted with a 9 week probation period; tuition is non-refundable.
